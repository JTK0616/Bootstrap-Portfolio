About Me

As an owner of Florida Indian River Groves, the leading provider of fresh fruit for school fundraisers, Jeff Knowles has assisted thousands of schools in raising millions of dollars for travel and general operating expenses.

Jeff manages the day-to-day operations and is actively involved in the company's new, e-commerce initiatives. As part of his ongoing committment to these initiatives, Jeff attends the University of Central Florida's Coding Bootcamp, where he is working to become a full stack web developer.

A graduate of the College of Business at the University of Central Florida, Jeff went on to earn an MBA degreee through the Rollins College Key Exectuive MBA program.

Jeff devotes his time to numerous community causes, including SafeSpace, Give Kids the World, and the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. An avid marathon runner, Jeff has run over 10 full marathons, many in support of the charities for which he volunteers.

Jeff resides in Vero Beach, Florida.
